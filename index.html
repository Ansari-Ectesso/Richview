About Us

Richview Developments, founded by industry veteran Nick Freda, represents a legacy of excellence in the construction and real estate sector. Our journey began in 2008, deeply rooted in Toronto's evolving urban landscape.

With over a decade of direct involvement in some of the city's landmark projects, Richview Developments stands at the forefront of shaping community-centric, sustainable urban spaces. Our approach combines Nick Freda's extensive experience with a strategic vision for the future.

Our Focus And Expertise

Our Strategy

Richview Developments has adopted a comprehensive strategy where we target land parcels already primed for development. In acquiring approved and ‘shovel-ready’ lands, we remove the development risk and focus strictly on assembling & managing the team for the construction, financing, and management of the completed asset. 

Our robust network, cultivated over 15 years, encompasses family and business contacts that enable us to acquire off-market approved lands and secure preferred rates and discounted government-backed bank financing for building high-quality rental apartment buildings.
